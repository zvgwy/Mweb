Fifth.

As I enter the final stretch of my college life, I find myself reflecting on the importance of discernment in my journey. Discernment has been a guiding light for me, helping me navigate through the uncertainties and challenges that come with this phase of life. It has taught me to pause, reflect, and make decisions that align with my values and aspirations. In this journal entry, I want to explore how discernment has been a crucial part of my growth and how it can continue to shape my path as I prepare to become a professional.

How can I grow more in my discernment? How can this help me as I make myself a better professional?

Discernment as a Path to Growth

Discernment is not about having all the answers right away, rather, it is about trusting the process of seeking them. For me, growing in discernment means learning how to pause and really think before I decide. I tend to overthink a lot, but not always in a helpful way. I want to shift that into something more intentional, like understanding why I’m choosing something and if it actually aligns with who I want to be. Discernment is not about having everything figured out, but about being honest with myself and trusting that clarity will come. With that, I am slowly learning that peace is often a sign that I am on the right path, even if it doesn’t look like everyone else’s journey.

As I prepare to become a professional, I can see how discernment can help me make better and thoughtful decisions. It can help me choose paths that will actually help me grow. I want to be someone who works with purpose and integrity. I know I’m still learning, and maybe that’s the point. Being a good professional is not just about skills, but about character. It can help me understand my strengths and weaknesses, helping me to stay grounded, especially when I feel pressured or uncertain. I trust that with discernment, consistency, and faith, I am slowly becoming the professional I am meant to be.
